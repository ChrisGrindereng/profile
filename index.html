About Me

The pace at which technology has evolved over the last few decades has been unparalleled. It has permeated every part of our world and has become an indispensable part of modern life. Due to its tremendous value, it is paramount that these technology systems are maintained and kept running. My work as an I.T. service professional is driven by a fascination for technology and a passion for problem solving. For 8 years I've worked in the Business Solutions and Managed I.T. Services industry with troubleshooting and implementing solutions to fit my clients needs and improving their relationship with the tech they depend on everyday. From working with large I.T. departments at University of Wyoming and University of Colorado to law offices, real estate brokerages and other small businesses, interfacing effectively with clients and understanding their needs is central to my success as a service provider. I believe communication is the foundation for cultivating strong relationships with clients and ensuring quick and effective technical support. With new technology solutions constantly on the market, it is important to provide clients with up to date systems to enhance their businesses. My enthusiasm for tech drives me continually learn about the newest technologies to enhance my skills and better serve my clients. The latest of these endeavors in education has been signing on with the Iron Yard as a student of web developement. As a recent transplant to Houston, I am excited to bring my skills to the area and look forward to undertaking new and exciting employment opportunities in working with complex network infrastructure.
